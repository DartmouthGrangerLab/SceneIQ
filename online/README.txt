Internet-based SceneIQ dataset

humanratings_5groups_nooutliers_final.csv contains the following:
Image - image name
dst_type - semantic category of image
dst_lev - degradation level:
  1 - 30% JPEG quality
  2 - 20% JPEG quality
  3 - 10% JPEG quality
  4 - 5%  JPEG quality
dmos_std - standard deviation of human ratings
dmos - human ratings
